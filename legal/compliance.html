Compliance

Last Updated: April 26, 2025

NOTICE: Wild Market Capital is an early-stage algorithmic trading entity. We are not a regulated financial institution, investment advisor, or broker-dealer. Our services are limited to algorithmic trading development and research.

Our Commitment

Wild Market Capital is committed to operating with integrity and transparency while navigating the evolving regulatory landscape of digital assets. This page outlines our approach to compliance and the measures we take to maintain ethical standards.

Regulatory Status

Wild Market Capital is currently operating as an early-stage trading firm focused on developing algorithmic strategies. We are not registered or licensed as a financial institution, investment advisor, or broker-dealer in any jurisdiction. Our current activities are focused on proprietary trading, research, and technology development.

We continuously monitor regulatory developments in the digital asset space across relevant jurisdictions and will pursue appropriate registrations or licenses if and when our activities require them.

Anti-Money Laundering (AML) & Know Your Customer (KYC)

While we do not currently provide services directly to the public that would necessitate formal AML/KYC procedures, we are committed to:

  • Conducting thorough due diligence on any potential partners, service providers, or collaborators
  • Maintaining detailed records of all significant transactions and business relationships
  • Refusing to engage with entities or individuals under international sanctions
  • Implementing appropriate compliance measures as our business evolves

Market Conduct

We are committed to fair and ethical market conduct. Our algorithmic trading strategies are designed to:

  • Avoid manipulative trading practices or market abuse
  • Engage in legitimate trading activities that contribute to market liquidity and efficiency
  • Operate with transparency regarding our activities to the extent possible without compromising proprietary strategies
  • Follow best practices and industry standards for algorithmic trading

Risk Management

We implement robust risk management practices including:

  • Maintaining appropriate position sizing and risk exposure limits
  • Implementing technical safeguards and circuit breakers in our algorithms
  • Regular testing and monitoring of our trading systems
  • Continuous assessment of market, operational, and regulatory risks

Jurisdictional Limitations

Due to varying regulatory requirements across different jurisdictions, certain services or collaborations may be restricted in particular regions. We assess jurisdictional requirements on a case-by-case basis.

Updates to Compliance Framework

As a growing entity operating in a rapidly evolving space, our compliance framework will develop alongside our business. We will update this policy as necessary to reflect changes in our operations, regulatory environment, or industry best practices.
